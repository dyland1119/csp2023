My portfolio

This is a cookie clicker game that I made in collaboration with Luca Rosano Doolittle. When you click on the moving cookie, you get a point for clicking it. After you get 20 cookies, you can purchase an automatic cookie clicker that will add 5 cookies for each rotation of the cookie. If you have 100 cookies, you can purchase an automatic cookie clicker that will add 10 cookies for each rotation.
