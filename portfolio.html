My portfolio

This is a cookie clicker game that I made in collaboration with Luca Rosano Doolittle. When you click on the moving cookie, you get a point for clicking it. After you get 20 cookies, you can purchase an automatic cookie clicker that will add 5 cookies for each rotation of the cookie.

If you have 100 cookies, you can purchase an automatic cookie clicker that will add 10 cookies for each rotation.

This is a game that is based on the classic game "Fruit Ninja" where you cut fruit and get points for it. If you do not hit the fruit, you do not get the points.
